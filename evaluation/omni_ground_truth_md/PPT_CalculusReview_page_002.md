# Slope

‚ÄĘ $Slope = rise/run$
‚ÄĘ $= \Delta y / \Delta x$
‚ÄĘ $ = (y_2 - y_1)/(x_2 - x_1)$
‚ÄĘ Order of points 1 and 2 not critical, but keeping them
  together is
‚ÄĘ Points may lie in any quadrant: slope will work out
‚ÄĘ Leibniz notation for derivative based on $\Delta y/\Delta x$; the derivative is written $dy/dx$
