例：求单位脉冲函数 $\delta ( t )$ 的拉氏变换。

$$
$$\delta \left( t\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{{t}_{0} \rightarrow  0}}\frac{1\left( t\right)  - 1\left( {t - {t}_{0}}\right) }{{t}_{0}}$$
$$

$$
$$L\left\lbrack  {\delta \left( t\right) }\right\rbrack   = {\int }_{0}^{\infty }\delta \left( t\right) {e}^{-{st}}{dt} = {\int }_{0}^{\infty }\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{{t}_{0} \rightarrow  0}}\frac{1\left( t\right)  - 1\left( {t - {t}_{0}}\right) }{{t}_{0}}{e}^{-{st}}{dt}$$
$$

$$
$$= \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{{t}_{0} \rightarrow  0}}\left\lbrack  {{\int }_{0}^{\infty }\frac{{e}^{-{st}}}{{t}_{0}}{dt} - \frac{1\left( {t - {t}_{0}}\right) {e}^{-s\left( {t - {t}_{0}}\right) }}{{t}_{0}}{e}^{-s{t}_{0}}{dt}}\right\rbrack   = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{{t}_{0} \rightarrow  0}}\frac{1 - {e}^{-{t}_{0}s}}{{t}_{0}s} = 1$$
$$
