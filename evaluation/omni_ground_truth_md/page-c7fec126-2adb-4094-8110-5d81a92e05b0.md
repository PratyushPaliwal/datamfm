Given the proof of Lemma A.1, it suffices to establish

(2) We expand $ {\bar{\alpha }}_{j,2} $ using the identity $ {\mathbf{R}}_{0} = {\mathbf{R}}_{0, - j} - {\mathbf{R}}_{0}\left( {{\mathbf{K}}_{0} - {\mathbf{K}}_{0, - j}}\right) {\mathbf{R}}_{0, - j} $:
