# What about color ?

■ Consistency is harder to achieve
\t– Gamut of devices much more variable
\t– Greater influence of psychovisual effects 
■ Extensive standards efforts e.g. ICC
■ DICOM recently defined color presentation
in a manner very similar to grayscale.
\t– Many display and print devices already have
ICC profiles, but few medical imaging devices
support color presentation state.
