# Derivative of a line

‚ÄĘ $y = mx + b$
‚ÄĘ slope $m$ and $y$ axis intercept $b$
‚ÄĘ derivative of $y = ax^n + b$ with respect to $x$:
‚ÄĘ $dy/dx = a ~n~ x^{(n-1)} $
‚ÄĘ Because $b$ is a constant -- think of it as $bx^0$ -- its
derivative is $0bx^{-1} = 0$
‚ÄĘ For a straight line, $a= m$ and $n= 1$ so
‚ÄĘ $dy/dx = m 1 x^{(0)}$, or because $x^0 = 1$,
‚ÄĘ $dy/dx = m$
