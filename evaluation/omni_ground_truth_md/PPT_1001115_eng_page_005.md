# Is Good English Important?

• For Individual
\t– Reading – lifelong learning to improve your
competiveness, especially when you want to acquire
the most up-to-date knowledge
\t– Writing – emails with foreign friends/co-workers/customers
\t– Listening - Q/A with foreign customers/boss/etc
\t– Speaking – presentation in front of foreign
customers/boss/etc, traveling abroad
• At University level
\t– Internationalization is one of the most prioritized
  tasks
